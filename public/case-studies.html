Real Estate

AI-Powered Lead Routing Cuts Response Time by 94%

94% Faster response
2.3x More conversions
0 Leads lost

Challenge: A real estate agency received leads from 6 different sources. Agents manually checked each source and responded hours later, losing deals to faster competitors.

Solution: We centralized all lead sources, used AI to qualify and score leads, and automatically assigned them to the right agent based on property type and availability. Instant SMS/email responses are sent while the agent is notified.

Result: Average response time dropped from 4 hours to 15 minutes. Lead conversion rate more than doubled.
