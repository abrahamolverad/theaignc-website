FAQ

Common Questions

What tools do you use to build automations?

We primarily use n8n (self-hosted or cloud), Make (formerly Integromat), and custom code when needed. For AI features, we integrate OpenAI, Claude, and other models. The choice depends on your requirements, budget, and whether you need self-hosted solutions for data privacy.

How long does it take to build an automation?

Simple automations (connecting 2-3 apps, linear workflow) take 1-2 weeks. Complex workflows with multiple branches, AI components, and error handling take 4-8 weeks. We'll give you an accurate timeline during the audit call.

What if something breaks after deployment?

All projects include 30 days of support. Retainer clients get ongoing maintenance. We also build error handling and notifications into every automation, so you'll know immediately if something fails. We typically respond within 4 business hours.

Can you work with our existing systems?

Yes. We've integrated with Salesforce, HubSpot, SAP, QuickBooks, Xero, Slack, Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, Shopify, WooCommerce, Airtable, Notion, and hundreds of other tools. If it has an API, we can connect it.
