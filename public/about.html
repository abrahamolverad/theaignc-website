About Us

Enterprise Expertise. Entrepreneur Pricing.

We're not freelancers who learned automation last month. We're corporate professionals who've spent 14+ years building systems at multinational companies.

Our Story

The AIgnc was founded with a simple observation: the automation tools that helped large corporations save millions were now accessible to everyone, but most small and medium businesses didn't know how to use them.

After 14+ years working in manufacturing, finance, sales, and operations at multinational corporations, we saw the same patterns everywhere: smart people spending hours on tasks that could be automated in minutes. Data entry. Report generation. Follow-up emails. Invoice processing. The list goes on.

We started The AIgnc to bring enterprise-level automation expertise to growing businesses. We speak the language of both business (understanding your actual needs) and technology (knowing how to build reliable solutions).

What Makes Us Different

  • We've been in your shoes. We've worked inside companies dealing with the same manual processes you're dealing with. We understand the real-world constraints, politics, and priorities.
  • We focus on outcomes, not tools. We don't care if we use n8n, Make, Zapier, or custom code. We care about solving your problem reliably and efficiently.
  • We build for the long term. Every automation includes error handling, logging, and documentation. When something breaks (and eventually it will), you'll know immediately and it'll be easy to fix.
  • We're honest about what automation can't do. Not everything should be automated. Some processes need human judgment. We'll tell you when automation isn't the right answer.
