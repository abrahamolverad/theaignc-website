Pricing FAQ

What determines the price of a project?

Project pricing depends on: (1) number of apps/systems to connect, (2) complexity of the logic (linear vs. branching), (3) whether AI/ML components are needed, and (4) data volume and error handling requirements. Simple automations (connecting 2-3 apps with straightforward logic) are on the lower end. Complex workflows with multiple branches, AI processing, and high reliability requirements are higher.

Are there additional costs for tools/software?

Sometimes. If we use n8n Cloud, Make, or Zapier, there are monthly subscription costs (typically $20-200/month depending on usage). If we self-host n8n, there's a server cost ($10-50/month). AI API costs (OpenAI, Claude) are passed through at cost. We'll always tell you about these costs upfront before starting.

What if I need changes after the project is done?

All projects include 30 days of support for bug fixes and minor adjustments. After that, changes can be done on an hourly basis ($150/hour) or you can move to a retainer for ongoing work. Many clients start with a project and then move to a retainer as they see results.

Can I cancel the retainer anytime?

Yes. We ask for 30 days notice so we can wrap up any in-progress work. You own everything we've built. We'll provide handoff documentation and make sure you're set up to maintain things yourself or with another provider.

Do you offer discounts for startups or non-profits?

We evaluate these on a case-by-case basis. If you're a startup with limited budget but an interesting automation challenge, or a non-profit doing meaningful work, reach out and let's talk. We can sometimes offer reduced rates or payment plans.
