Workflow Automation

Connect your apps and automate multi-step processes. When something happens in one system, automatically trigger actions in others.

What is Workflow Automation?

Workflow automation uses software to execute tasks automatically based on predefined rules. Instead of manually copying data between apps, sending follow-up emails, or updating spreadsheets, automation handles these tasks instantly and without errors.

At The AIgnc, we build custom automation workflows using tools like n8n, Make (formerly Integromat), and custom code when needed. We connect your existing business tools and create seamless data flows between them.

Common Workflow Automations We Build

Lead Management Automation

When a new lead fills out your website form:

  • Automatically add them to your CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive)
  • Send a personalized welcome email
  • Notify your sales team via Slack or email
  • Add them to your email marketing sequence
  • Create a follow-up task for 3 days later

Result: Leads get responded to in under 5 minutes instead of hours. No leads fall through the cracks.

Invoice Processing Automation

When an invoice arrives in your inbox:

  • Extract vendor name, amount, due date, and line items
  • Create an entry in your accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero)
  • Match against purchase orders
  • Route for approval if over a threshold
  • Send payment reminders before due dates

Result: Invoice processing time drops from 15 minutes to under 1 minute. No data entry errors.

E-commerce Order Fulfillment

When an order is placed on your store:

  • Update inventory across all sales channels
  • Generate shipping label and tracking number
  • Send order confirmation to customer
  • Notify warehouse team
  • Update your reporting dashboard

Result: Orders ship same-day. Inventory is always accurate across channels.

Employee Onboarding Automation

When HR marks a new hire in your system:

  • Create accounts in all required tools (Slack, Google Workspace, etc.)
  • Add to correct groups and channels
  • Send welcome email with first-day instructions
  • Create calendar invites for orientation meetings
  • Assign training tasks in your project management tool

Result: New hires are fully set up on day one. IT saves 2+ hours per new employee.

Reporting Automation

On a schedule (daily, weekly, monthly):

  • Pull data from multiple sources (CRM, accounting, analytics)
  • Calculate KPIs and metrics
  • Generate formatted report
  • Send to stakeholders via email or Slack
  • Update your dashboard

Result: Reports arrive automatically. No more Monday morning data scrambles.
