AI Integration

Add intelligence to your workflows. AI that reads, writes, classifies, and makes decisions based on your business rules.

What is AI Integration?

AI integration means embedding artificial intelligence capabilities into your existing business workflows. Instead of just moving data between apps, AI can understand that data, make decisions, and generate new content.

We integrate models from OpenAI (GPT-4), Anthropic (Claude), Google (Gemini), and custom fine-tuned models depending on your needs, budget, and privacy requirements.

AI Capabilities We Implement

AI Chatbots & Virtual Assistants

Custom chatbots that answer questions using your knowledge base:

  • Website chat that handles customer support queries 24/7
  • Internal helpdesk bot for employee questions
  • Sales qualification bot that books meetings
  • Trained on your documentation, FAQs, and product info
  • Escalation to humans when needed

Result: 70% of support queries handled without human intervention. Response time drops to seconds.

Document Processing & Data Extraction

AI that reads and extracts information from documents:

  • Invoice data extraction (vendor, amount, line items, due date)
  • Contract analysis and key term extraction
  • Resume parsing for recruitment
  • Form data extraction from scanned documents
  • Receipt processing for expense management

Result: Documents that took 10 minutes to process manually are done in seconds with 95%+ accuracy.

Email Classification & Routing

AI that understands incoming emails and routes them appropriately:

  • Classify emails by intent (support, sales, billing, spam)
  • Route to the correct team or queue
  • Prioritize based on urgency and customer tier
  • Draft suggested replies for common questions
  • Extract action items and create tasks

Result: Support tickets get to the right person immediately. First-response time improves by 80%.

Content Generation

AI that generates content following your brand voice:

  • Product descriptions from specifications
  • Social media post drafts
  • Email sequence copywriting
  • Meeting summaries and action items
  • Report narratives from data

Result: Content that took hours to write is drafted in minutes. Humans review and refine.

Intelligent Classification & Tagging

AI that categorizes and tags data automatically:

  • CRM lead scoring and qualification
  • Support ticket categorization
  • Product review sentiment analysis
  • Content tagging for searchability
  • Transaction categorization for accounting

Result: Data is consistently categorized without human review. Better reporting and searchability.

Privacy & Data Handling

We understand that AI integration raises privacy concerns. Here's how we address them:

  • Data minimization: We only send the minimum required data to AI models
  • Self-hosted options: For sensitive data, we can deploy open-source models on your infrastructure
  • API agreements: We use enterprise API tiers where OpenAI/Anthropic don't train on your data
  • Audit trails: All AI interactions can be logged for compliance
