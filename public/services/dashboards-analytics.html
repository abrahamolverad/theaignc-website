Dashboards & Analytics

See your business data in real-time. Automated reports that arrive without asking. Alerts when metrics go out of range.

The Problem with Manual Reporting

Most businesses waste hours every week pulling data from different systems, copying it into spreadsheets, and formatting reports. By the time the report is ready, the data is already old.

We build automated data pipelines and dashboards that pull data from your systems in real-time, so you always have current information without the manual work.

What We Build

Real-Time Dashboards

Custom dashboards that show your key metrics at a glance:

  • Sales dashboards (pipeline, revenue, conversion rates)
  • Operations dashboards (order status, inventory levels, fulfillment)
  • Marketing dashboards (campaign performance, lead sources, ROI)
  • Financial dashboards (cash flow, expenses, profitability)
  • Support dashboards (ticket volume, response times, satisfaction)

Result: Know what's happening in your business without asking anyone or running reports.

Automated Reports

Reports that generate and deliver themselves on schedule:

  • Daily sales summaries delivered to Slack at 6 PM
  • Weekly performance reports emailed to leadership every Monday
  • Monthly financial summaries generated for board meetings
  • Quarterly trend analysis with comparisons to previous periods
  • Custom reports triggered by events (deal closed, campaign ended, etc.)

Result: Reports arrive automatically. No more spending Monday morning compiling data.

Smart Alerts & Notifications

Get notified when something needs your attention:

  • Alert when a key metric drops below threshold
  • Notification when a big deal moves to a new stage
  • Warning when inventory is running low
  • Alert when support tickets spike
  • Notification when campaigns are underperforming

Result: You only pay attention when something actually needs attention.

Data Integration & Pipelines

Connect data from multiple sources into a single view:

  • Combine CRM, accounting, and marketing data
  • Standardize data formats across systems
  • Create calculated fields and custom metrics
  • Build data warehouses for historical analysis
  • Set up ETL pipelines that run automatically

Result: One source of truth instead of scattered data in different tools.

Dashboard Tools We Use

We build dashboards using the tool that fits your needs:

  • Google Looker Studio (Data Studio): Free, easy to share, good for simpler dashboards
  • Metabase: Open source, self-hosted option, good balance of power and simplicity
  • Tableau / Power BI: For complex enterprise requirements
  • Custom web dashboards: When you need something highly specific or embedded in your app
